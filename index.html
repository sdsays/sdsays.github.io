About śaṅkaradāsa

A pen-name. It is composite of śaṅkara + dāsa, meaning servant of Absolute Auspiciousness.

As long as duality remains
The idea of separation remains
Let remain individual
A servant to Universal

Quick takeaway

Purpose of human life is to wake up to Conscious Self. The world as we see is merely superimposed on it like snake on a rope. That's where modern physics is also converging to. Path to this realisation is:

Serve. Love. Give. Purify. Meditate. Realise. – Swami Sivananda
