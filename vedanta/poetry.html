Poetry

1. Chorus

No weight of life
No wait of death
A fallen Grace
In Your embrace
Breathing Love
All pores porous
Singing union
All winds chorus

2. Verses

Inscripted on our heart
Love primordial verses
She recites
With enchanting magic
and I Witness

3. Melody

A pointed finger
Between my brows —
To attain equipoise
In worldly noise
Way is to Renounce
Said Guru's voice
But i was weak
and paid no heed
That sunlit path
i tread now
Desires do arise
But merely as comedy
The song of soul
Is unfading melody

4. Whisper

Again!
Inner whisper
But old victim
of this fraud
Hard to judge
Who spoke
Demon or God
—
Desirous mind can't really tell.

5. Doubt

It perishes journey
It perishes relation
It perishes a being
Just a doubt -
When it sprouts

6. Drowsy

In dream, dear soul
By which light do you see?
So stay alert with That
In world when you go drowsy
That light is Awareness
Ever shining in its bareness

7. Bring toils to fruition

Do it —
For mother's labor
For father's sweat
For Guru's hope
For lover's eyes
This sacrifice, to
Bring toils to fruition
A seeker's volition
